Preconference Workshop

This workshop is organized in collaboration with the eScience Center and the Open Science Community Tilburg.

Teachers: MSc Rasoul Norouzi (Tilburg University), dr. Erik Tjong Kim Sang (eScience Center)

For our preconference workshop, we partnered with the eScience Center NL. Thanks to their expertise, you can now learn four main skills:

  1. Automatically downloading published papers in bulk for systematic review, text mining, bibliometric analysis, etc., using the COSMAS tool developed by the eScience Center
  2. Constructing theories based on causal claims in published papers, downloaded using the COSMAS tool
  3. Harmonizing social science concepts (e.g., recognizing that “sleep disorder” and “insomnia” refer to the same concept)
  4. Knowledge extraction of other relevant information from published papers using specialized models with known validity and reliability (so, not ChatGPT) - like p-values, sample sizes, etc.

Submission formats

Keynotes

An invited talk of approximately 40 minutes, followed by 20 minutes of discussion.

Submitted Talks

This format is a 20 minute talk, plus 10 minute discussion or Q&A. Anyone can submit a talk like this, and multiple presenters can share the stage to present a join project or discuss opposing views. Optionally, indicate whether your submitted talk should be considered for a Lightning Talk or Poster slot, if it is not selected for a Submitted Talk.

Lightning Talks

This format is a 5 minute elevator pitch designed to inspire and spark discussion. Speakers can present a paper, idea, software, or other resource.

Poster

There is a 1.5 hour slot for poster presentations, during which there is a walking lunch in the same room to stimulate conversations.

Participation Costs

Expected: 100-140 euros

This covers participation in all sessions, catering during the conference days, and participation in the conference dinner.

We want to be inclusive, so the registration form will include an option to apply for a registration fee waiver if you do not have a conference budget, optionally in exchange for volunteering at the conference.

Waivers will be awarded subject to our financial position and the number of applicants.
